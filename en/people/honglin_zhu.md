desp: "Honglin Zhu received her MB (Clinical Medicine) from University of South China, MM (Pathophysiology) from School of Basic Medical Sciences, Central South University, and PhD (Internal Medicine) from Xiangya Hospital, Central South University. She was a Postdoctoral Fellow at Department of Rheumatology, University Hospital Erlangen, FAU, Germany (2018–2020) and a Visiting Scholar at Department of Immunology, UT Southwestern Medical Center, Dallas, USA (2014–2015). She is currently Associate Research Fellow and Attending Physician at Xiangya Hospital, CSU. Her research focuses on refractory autoimmune diseases (IIM/SSc): decoding the immune microenvironment, defining pathogenic cell subtypes, and translating findings into precision classification, prognostic biomarkers and therapeutic targets. She has published in Ann Rheum Dis., J Cachexia Sarcopenia Muscle, J Invest Dermatol., J Autoimmun., Arthritis Rheumatol., and Metabolism, among others, and collaborates internationally with Prof. Jörg Distler (University Hospital Düsseldorf). She is Editorial Board Member of Therapeutic Advances in Musculoskeletal Disease and received the Edith Busch Award (IFSS) in 2024."
